Investing 101

What is Investing?

Investing means putting your money into assets — like stocks, bonds, or funds — with the goal of growing it over time. When you buy a stock, you own a tiny piece of a company. If the company does well, your investment grows.

⚠️ Understanding Risk

All investments carry risk. Prices go up and down. You could lose some or all of what you invest. The key is to never invest money you can't afford to lose, and to diversify — don't put all your eggs in one basket.

💰 How Profits Work

You make money by buying low and selling high. If you buy a share at £10 and sell at £15, you've made £5 profit. But if it drops to £7, you'd lose £3 if you sell. Patience and strategy are essential.
